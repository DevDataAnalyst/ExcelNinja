1. Copy the code
2. Open MS Word
3. View tab -> Macros button -> "View Macros" option
4. Write a dummy Macros name. E.g. test
5. Choose "Normal.dotm (Global template)"
6. Click on "Create" button
7. Delete the dummy Macros
8. Paste the code of Step 1 above. 
9. Code automatically saved.
10. Close the Macros window.
11. Go to QAT (top left corner of the window)
12. Choose "More Commands"
13. Choose "Macros" instead of "Popular Commands"
14. Choose our Macro (NoMoreDeleteSpace)
15. Click on the button "Add"
16. Click on "Modify" to choose the icon. Press OK.

17. Keep cursor at the beginning of the document (Ctrl Home)
18. Click on the Macros icon
